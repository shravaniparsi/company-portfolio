What Is Amateur Robotics Club(ARC)?

Amateur Robotics Club(ARC) is a motive powered by Alternate Dimension to spread knowledge about Robotics. ARC intends students to learn Robotics from the scratch. We are also providing workshops, year long programs both in Schools and Colleges. We started providing internships and trainings in colleges and individuals who are searching for jobs.

ROBOTICS

Our teaching technique is very unique . our specialty lies in making students learn Robotics from scratch. Making them learn designing, printing their own design using 3D printer, programming, sensors and assembling which makes them learn how a robot is completely made. We also tech them how to make a robot from scrap. Learning Robotics also increase creative thinking, decision making and also team work. .

3D SCANNING

We help you to learn how to 3D scan the objects . 3D scanning captures the 3D image of an object.

3D Designing

3D Printing

Learn printing your own 3D object from a computer aided design model which prints layer by layer of the designed model.
